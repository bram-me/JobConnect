AFRICA`S GROWTH PROSPECTS

Africa's growth potential is diverse and promising, particularly in sectors that can empower young job seekers. JobConnect is dedicated to bridging the gap between this potential and the skills of the youth.

For the global economy to grow faster, African governments, in partnership with the private sector, must act to capitalize on Africa’s youth and dynamism..
1 OF 4

• Technology and Innovation: Thriving tech hubs in cities like Lagos, Nairobi, and Cape Town are fostering startups. Fintech innovations, such as M-Pesa in Kenya, are transforming financial services and creating new job opportunities. JobConnect will provide targeted training programs to equip young people with essential tech skills, preparing them for careers in this booming sector.

2 OF 4

• E-commerce: Platforms like Jumia and Konga are revolutionizing retail, making online shopping accessible. This sector provides a platform for young entrepreneurs to engage in the digital economy. JobConnect will offer courses on e-commerce strategies, helping youth launch and manage their online businesses effectively.

3 OF 4

• Agriculture: Contributing 23% to the GDP and employing over 60% of the population, agriculture offers significant opportunities. JobConnect will focus on providing modern agricultural training and resources, enabling youth to enhance productivity and sustainability in farming.

4 OF 4

• Banking: African banks are evolving to meet client needs with expanded offerings like credit cards and overdraft facilities, fostering opportunities for young professionals. JobConnect will facilitate training in financial literacy and banking skills, preparing young job seekers for careers in this essential sector.
